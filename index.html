Karnataka Ultimate Players Association


On Feb 22 2011, Karnataka Ultimate Players Association(KUPA) was successfully registered with the Govt of Karnataka. The organization was formed primarily to be able to take up various Ultimate initiatives with the Govt of Karnataka. It would help us procure better ground facilities for the tournaments.

In the coming days KUPA also aims toward facilitating Ultimate training for new teams and helping build new teams across the state. Long term plans for the sport also include taking the sport directly to schools and colleges.
