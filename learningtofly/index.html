Learning to Fly, or L2F as the team is commonly called, is one of the oldest Ultimate teams in the country. Although they were introduced to Ultimate around February 2008, they had been playing other forms of the game since well before. But once this group was introduced to Ultimate, they were hooked. They learnt almost everything together as a team, right from throwing a flick to building on stratergies. Most other teams consider this to be one of the main reasons for L2F's success on field.

Over the years L2F has shown good performance consistently in each tournament, almost always figuring among the top teams in every tournament.

Checkout their website for more details about this team: www.learningtofly.in
