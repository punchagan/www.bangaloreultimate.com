Team Falcons is one mans vision, SL, as he is known in the Ultimate circles, shouldered the responsibility to introduce the sport to a bunch of college kids at the Loyola school Bannerghatta rd. The kids from this school covered ground from not knowing the game to finishing second in the biggest tournament in a little over a year.

In the very first tournament they participated, they finished on top of the the silver pool(2011 Flybaba, Kodai). In the very next tournament in Bangalore they placed Sixth in final standings. the next time they played in Bangalore this team went on to finish Second in the final standings. Beating some of the toughest competition in the country.

At present, Falcons is one of the most formidable teams in the country. Some of the best teams in the country have a tough time keeping up with thier speed and flow. Falcons are proof that dedication and hardwork is always well rewarded.
